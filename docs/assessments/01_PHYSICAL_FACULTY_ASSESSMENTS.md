## Competency 1: Physical Fitness & Endurance

**Definition**: Ability to sustain physical activity and maintain cardiovascular health.

### Questions

1. **How many days per week do you engage in moderate to vigorous physical activity?**
   - Type: Likert Scale
   - Options: Never (0), 1-2 days (1), 3-4 days (2), 5-6 days (3), Daily (4)
   - Correct Answer: 5-6 days or Daily
   - Explanation: WHO recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate activity per week

2. **What is the recommended heart rate zone for cardiovascular fitness training?**
   - Type: Multiple Choice
   - Options: A) 40-50% max HR, B) 50-70% max HR, C) 70-85% max HR, D) 90-100% max HR
   - Correct Answer: C
   - Explanation: Target heart rate zone for fitness is 70-85% of maximum heart rate

3. **You need to climb 5 flights of stairs. How do you feel?**
   - Type: Self-Assessment
   - Options: Very difficult (0), Difficult (1), Moderate (2), Easy (3), Very easy (4)
   - Scoring: Higher scores indicate better endurance

4. **How long can you sustain moderate-intensity exercise without stopping?**
   - Type: Multiple Choice
   - Options: A) <10 min, B) 10-20 min, C) 20-40 min, D) 40+ min
   - Correct Answer: D (optimal)
   - Explanation: Good cardiovascular fitness allows 40+ minutes of sustained activity

5. **After climbing stairs, how quickly does your heart rate return to normal?**
   - Type: Multiple Choice
   - Options: A) <1 min, B) 1-2 min, C) 2-5 min, D) >5 min
   - Correct Answer: A or B
   - Explanation: Faster recovery indicates better cardiovascular fitness

6. **How often do you experience shortness of breath during daily activities?**
   - Type: Likert Scale
   - Options: Always (0), Often (1), Sometimes (2), Rarely (3), Never (4)
   - Correct Answer: Rarely or Never
   - Explanation: Good fitness means minimal breathlessness during normal activities

7. **Can you complete a 30-minute brisk walk without stopping?**
   - Type: True/False
   - Correct Answer: True
   - Explanation: Basic fitness standard for adults

8. **What is your resting heart rate?**
   - Type: Multiple Choice
   - Options: A) >100 bpm, B) 80-100 bpm, C) 60-80 bpm, D) <60 bpm
   - Correct Answer: C or D
   - Explanation: Lower resting heart rate indicates better cardiovascular fitness

9. **How do you feel after 20 minutes of moderate exercise?**
   - Type: Self-Assessment
   - Options: Exhausted (0), Very tired (1), Moderately tired (2), Slightly tired (3), Energized (4)
   - Scoring: Higher scores indicate better fitness

10. **Do you regularly participate in activities that increase your heart rate?**
    - Type: Likert Scale
    - Options: Never (0), Rarely (1), Sometimes (2), Often (3), Always (4)
    - Correct Answer: Often or Always
    - Explanation: Regular cardiovascular activity is essential for fitness

11. **Scenario: You're late for a meeting on the 3rd floor. Do you take stairs or elevator?**
    - Type: Scenario-Based
    - Options: A) Always elevator, B) Usually elevator, C) Usually stairs, D) Always stairs
    - Correct Answer: C or D
    - Explanation: Choosing stairs demonstrates fitness confidence

12. **How long does it take you to recover after intense physical activity?**
    - Type: Multiple Choice
    - Options: A) >24 hours, B) 12-24 hours, C) 6-12 hours, D) <6 hours
    - Correct Answer: C or D
    - Explanation: Faster recovery indicates better fitness
